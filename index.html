What We Do

Combat

There are a ton of PVP opportunities for our pilots. We live near Jita, this means there are plently of heavily populated low security systems nearby for us. We do small-gang and medium-gang roams. There is a piece for everyone to enjoy.

Industry

In EVE, Industry is the part of the game that handles the transformation of raw materials into items that fuel the war machine in this beautiful game. Without Industry, there are no ships to kill and without war, there is no demand for new stuff.

Exploration

Come with us to the remote corners or New Eden to find mystic landscapes of long lost civilizations filled with priceless loot and often guarded by ancient but deadly AI technology waiting to be discovered.

What We Offer

Ship Replacement

Corporation-wide ship replacement is available for our members taking part in corporation fleets.

Transportation

Transportation is available to and from highsec trading hubs (Jita, Amarr). Members in our corporation do freighter runs to resupply our home base. Let one of them know if you want something hauled.

Freindly Community

We know that real life comes first. You will never be pestered for mandatory fleet ops. Play at your own pace. Enjoy.

How to join us:

  1. Join our public in-game channel, "Sample corp public" or join our Discord. Speak with any recruiters if you have any questions. If you think this is the corp for you, continue on.
  2. Wait for the join go-ahead from a Recruiter.
  3. Quit any previous corporations.
  4. Dock-up in any station. Press Alt-E, search "A sample corp".
  5. Click "Apply to Corporation" on the show info screen.
  6. If accepted, you will be receive full access to our discord server.
